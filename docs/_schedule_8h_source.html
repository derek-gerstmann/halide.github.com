15 /** A reference to a site in a Halide statement at the top of the
16  * body of a particular for loop. Evaluating a region of a halide
17  * function is done by generating a loop nest that spans its
18  * dimensions. We schedule the inputs to that function by
19  * recursively injecting realizations for them at particular sites
20  * in this loop nest. A LoopLevel identifies such a site. */

30  LoopLevel() {}

45  * loop, to see if this loop level refers to the site
